It is with great pleasure and delight that I welcome you to Deoudone Empowerment Foundation, an initiative on the mission to empower underprivileged the youths, less privilege and women through relevant education, skills development, innovative healthcare and focused livelihood programs.

This we have done over the past years and have recorded significant impact through various activities that have been beneficial to many youths, the less privilege and women.

As founder and visioner, inspite of the many challenges we are faced with as a not-forprofit organisation, the vision still remains clear in sight and we continually do our best to make sure these underprivileged women are empowered, upskilled and uplifted.

I welcome you on this journey and look forward to your consideration as a partner to this noble cause.
